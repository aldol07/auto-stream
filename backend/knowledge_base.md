# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## About AutoStream
AutoStream is a SaaS platform that provides automated video editing tools for content creators. It uses AI to help YouTubers, Instagram creators, and other video producers edit videos faster and smarter.

## Pricing Plans

### Basic Plan
- Price: $29/month
- Videos: 10 videos/month
- Resolution: Up to 720p
- Features: Auto-cut, background music sync, basic transitions
- Support: Email support (business hours)

### Pro Plan
- Price: $79/month
- Videos: Unlimited videos/month
- Resolution: Up to 4K
- Features: AI captions, auto-cut, background music sync, advanced transitions, brand kit, priority rendering
- Support: 24/7 live support

## Company Policies

### Refund Policy
- Refunds are available within 7 days of purchase
- No refunds after 7 days
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days

### Support Policy
- Basic Plan: Email support during business hours (Mon-Fri, 9AM-6PM EST)
- Pro Plan: 24/7 live chat and priority email support

### Billing
- All plans are billed monthly
- Annual billing available with 20% discount
- Cancel anytime, no long-term commitment

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I upgrade from Basic to Pro?
Yes, you can upgrade anytime. The price difference will be prorated for the remaining days in your billing cycle.

### What video formats does AutoStream support?
AutoStream supports MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, and WebM formats.

### Is there a free trial?
Yes, AutoStream offers a 7-day free trial on the Pro plan. No credit card required.

### Can I use AutoStream for Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts?
Absolutely! AutoStream is optimized for all major platforms including YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and more.

### How does AI captioning work?
Our AI caption feature automatically transcribes and syncs captions to your video in 30+ languages. Available on the Pro plan only.
